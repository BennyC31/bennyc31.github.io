Data Visualization Pages

Welcome to my GitHub page! The main purpose is to share visualizations created during Penn Data Science Boot Camp and any personal visualizations. My main interests are in sports data, which is also the majority of data I have collected over the years. With the knowledge I gained and years of experiences working with data I have decided to start publishing this information for others to enjoy.

The Earthquakes visualization was modified from one of Penn's challenges. I thought it might be an interesting map to share.
